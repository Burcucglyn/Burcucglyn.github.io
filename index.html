Featured Work

Things I've built.

London Air Quality Prediction ↗

MSc dissertation. Trained 556 models (Random Forest and CNN) across London's air quality monitoring networks: LAQN (78 stations, 6 regulatory pollutants) and DEFRA (18 stations, 37 pollutants). Compared both networks against shared regulatory pollutants, with an extended comparison across DEFRA's full pollutant set. Built a three stage cleaning pipeline that reduced LAQN metadata issues from 47.5% to 17.2%. The key finding from the Random Forest models: t minus 1 lag features explain 85 to 95% of prediction variance. Hourly air quality prediction is fundamentally a persistence problem, and data quality (DEFRA, 91.2% complete) consistently beat data quantity (LAQN, 141 site pollutant combinations).

Python scikit-learn TensorFlow Pandas
AudioPulse Pipeline ↗

Data engineering pipeline for YouTube audio collection and engagement analysis. Built serial, parallel, and rate limited download modes with thread safe logging. Parallel mode ran 5x faster. Ran identical queries in both Pandas and PySpark to compare behaviour on the same dataset.

RESTful social platform with JWT auth, post expiration logic, and interaction tracking. Deployed three ways: VM with PM2, Docker with docker-compose, and Kubernetes on GKE (5 replicas, load balancer). 20 automated tests, 100% pass rate.

Speech Classifier NLP ↗

Two NLP projects: syntactic analysis of 19th century novels using dependency parsing and PMI, and a political speech classifier trained on 40,000 UK parliamentary speeches. SVM with TF IDF outperformed the SMOTE pipeline, a valid finding about when simpler wins.

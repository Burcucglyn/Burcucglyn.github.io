Beyond Work

The rest of the picture.

Work is one part. This is everything else.

I'm Turkish, living in London for the past six years. I came here for a different pace and stayed because the city keeps delivering it. Most of my time outside of work is spent moving — in water, on walls, or in the gym. The rest splits between the kitchen, a book, and finding somewhere new to eat.

Swimming

Competitive swimmer. This is the constant — it's been part of my life longer than anything else on this page. I train regularly and it's where my head is clearest. The pool is my version of a reset button.

Bouldering

Currently climbing V3/V4 problems. I like bouldering because every route is a small puzzle — you read the wall, plan the sequence, and either the beta works or it doesn't. No ambiguity.

Strength Training

Consistent part of the routine. I train to support swimming and climbing, but also because I like the process of tracking progress and adjusting variables over time. Not unlike tuning hyperparameters, honestly.

Cooking

I cook the way I approach most things — figure out the fundamentals, then improvise. Turkish food is the foundation. London's ingredient access makes everything else possible.

Food

I take eating seriously. Finding the right restaurant, the unexpected neighbourhood spot, the place that does one thing properly — that's a good day. London is unbeatable for this.

Reading

I read across topics rather than sticking to one genre. Whatever pulls me in. The best books change how I think about something I thought I already understood.

Music

Always on. Whether I'm working, training, or cooking — music sets the pace. My taste is wide and I'm not precious about it.

Outdoors

Nature is where I go when London gets too loud. Hiking, coast walks, anywhere with open space. Turkey has mountains and coastline that still surprise me every time I go back.

Travelling ties most of this together. I've lived in enough places to know that the best way to understand somewhere is through the food, the landscape, and how people spend their time when they're not working. That's what I look for wherever I go.
